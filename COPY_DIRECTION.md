# Afro Arc Copy Direction

## Voice
The writing should sound:
- bold
- elevated
- intelligent
- cinematic
- future-facing
- controlled
- emotionally charged

## Avoid
- generic startup language
- vague hype
- corny epic language
- excessive fluff
- sounding like a template

## Hero Copy Direction
Headline should feel like:
- entering a universe
- stepping into something larger than a brand
- future mythology, media, and imagination

## Manifesto Direction
Write Afro Arc as:
- a signal
- a studio
- a universe engine
- a creative force building stories, symbols, and worlds

## CTA Direction
Should feel like an invitation into a larger world, not a bland signup request.
