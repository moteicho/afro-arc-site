# Afro Arc Brand Guide

## Brand Essence
Afro Arc is a multimedia universe-building company focused on powerful storytelling, mythic scale, future-facing imagination, and culturally rich creative identity.

## Brand Energy
- bold
- futuristic
- mythic
- cinematic
- intelligent
- expansive
- mysterious
- premium
- emotionally charged

## Visual Direction
The brand should feel like a fusion of:
- high-end entertainment studio
- cinematic sci-fi
- African futurism
- mythology
- premium editorial design
- elite modern motion design

## Color Direction
Explore a palette around:
- obsidian / near-black
- midnight blue
- electric blue
- gold
- violet
- emerald accents
- subtle glowing neutrals

## Typography Direction
Should feel:
- modern
- bold
- premium
- cinematic
- editorial
- sharp
- memorable

## Motion Direction
Animation should feel:
- smooth
- intentional
- atmospheric
- premium
- layered
- story-supporting
- never chaotic

## Emotional Goal
The site should make people feel:
- awe
- intrigue
- scale
- possibility
- creative authority
- curiosity about the universe

## Copy Tone
- visionary
- intelligent
- mythic
- concise
- cinematic
- clear
- not corny
- not generic
